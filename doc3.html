Prof. Dr. Ahmed Al-Moatasem

Prof. Dr. Ahmed Al-Moatasem, LASIK and Eye Surgery Consultant Professor at the Faculty of Medicine - Ain Shams University Arab doctor
Consultant Ophthalmologist and Vision Correction Surgeon in the Arab Republic of Egypt, the United Arab Emirates, the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and Libya.
Dr. Ahmed Al-Moatasem obtained fellowship, training and residency in Great Britain and America where he worked as an ophthalmologist and was the first ophthalmologist to perform ring operations for the treatment of keratoconus in America, Egypt and the Emirates for more than 20 years. He was also a pioneer in introducing the Implantable Lenses technology as well as laser surgery and cataract surgery.
He has published many research papers on corneal diseases and their progress in Europe and America, which have been published in medical journals. Biography of Dr. Ahmed Al-Moatasem
Dr. Ahmed received his Fellowship in Ophthalmology from the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh, UK. Furthermore, in 2002, he obtained his Doctorate in Ophthalmology from the USA and Egypt.
In 2002, he moved to Cairo, where he was appointed as a lecturer of ophthalmology at the Faculty of Medicine and then obtained the degree of Professor of Ophthalmology at the Faculty of Medicine, Ain Shams University in 2017.
Dr. Ahmed Al-Moatasem has published many articles on the analysis and management of eye diseases. Dr. Ahmed Al-Moatasem has been invited from many countries in the Middle East to give (PRK LASTK & IntraLASTK) training courses in laser eye surgery to doctors.
Fellowship and membership Fellow of the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh (RCS) Member of the American Academy of Ophthalmology - San Francisco Member of the International Society of Refractive Surgery Professor of Ophthalmology, Faculty of Medicine - Ain Shams University.
