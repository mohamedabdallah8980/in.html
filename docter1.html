Dr. Mazen Fakhry

Dr. Mazen Mohamed Fakhry is a professor of neurosurgery and spine surgery at the Faculty of Medicine, Alexandria University. He has over 30 years of experience in this field. His medical services include peripheral nerve surgery, pediatric and adult neurosurgery, brain endoscopy, and scoliosis correction.

Academic certificates and scientific qualifications:

Master's degree in Neurosurgery.
PhD in Neurosurgery and Spine.
